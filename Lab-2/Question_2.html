Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is an academic subject comprising
approaches of computer science and computer engineering. There is no clear
division in computing between science and engineering, just like in the
field of materials science and engineering. However, some classes are historically
more related to computer science (e.g. data structures and algorithms),
and other to computer engineering (e.g. computer architecture).
